Hours & Rates

Monday thru Thursday: 6:30 pm – 10:00 pm
Friday: CLOSED (Volunteers only)
Saturday: 11:00 am – 5:00 pm
Sunday: 12:00 pm – 3:00 pm (SUMMER HOURS)

We are also closed on the following holidays:
New Year’s Day, Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Eve/Day.

Workshop Rates:

This includes all tools, lubes, oil, grease, degreasers, polishes, sprays, gloves if you’d like them, rags, an apron, access to bike repair literature, and guidance from one of our skilled members.

$7 Per Hour

$4 Per Half Hour

We accept Cash money, Discover, Master Card, Visa, debit cards ($10 minimum for cards please), and personal checks. Please have a photo I.D.

A sliding scale is available for those who cannot afford our fee. Please let us know if that’s the case when you arrive at the shop and we can work with you rather than discussing it when your project is finished.

Also Available

$100 Annual Unlimited Card ($80 for High School / College Students or Seniors with proper ID) Unlimited use of all tools, lubes & cleaners in the shop, access to bike repair literature, guidance from one of our skilled volunteers.  All for ONE year.  Sweet!

$55 for 10 Hours Card – If you’re building a frame up from scratch or have a project you know will take some time, buy in bulk, and save fifteen dollars compared to the usual $70 for 10 hours.

Santa Monica College Students with a current or future semester Associated Student sticker on their ID card get FREE shop stand time!

Parking: We have at least 5 LADOT issued bike racks out front.  There is metered street parking on Venice Blvd. from 8 am until 8 pm, Monday thru Saturday.  Free parking on Sunday.  Hourly parking til 6pm on Ocean View Ave.  There is limited parking behind the Bikerowave.

The meter maidens prowl by constantly and are ruthless in their ticket writing.  Their eyes are dead with purpose, like that of the Great White– Ye be warned!

Location:  12255 Venice Blvd. Los Angeles CA 90066. We are located between Centinela Ave. and Grand View Blvd. on the North side of Venice Blvd.  We are across the street from LABrakeless and Venice Grind .   And right between Time Warp and Good Hurt.  Come for brunch at the Mar Vista Farmer’s Market on Sundays 9 am ’til 2 pm, then come over and get your hands dirty!
