Get Involved

Become a volunteer at the illustrious, no longer obscure, awesome, yet still humble, biggest little community bike co-op in the Milky Way* ;

  • Do you want to be an active part of the growing community of bicyclists in greater LA?
  • Do you want to know how to fix a flat, build a wheel, rebuild your bottom bracket, or put together a bike from a box of parts?
  • Do you like hanging out with fun and interesting (ok, weird) people who all love bicycles?

Then boy do we have the gig for you!

Volunteering

Give a little time, learn some skills, help your community. There is no minimum time requirement to be a volunteer, any help is appreciated. It’s really easy too:

Come in to the shop Monday-Thursday at 6:30pm (maybe even a few minutes early) and introduce yourself to the Head Mechanic. They will give you a short Orientation and all the details. No prior knowledge of bike mechanics or the Bikerowave required!

Membership

If you find yourself Volunteering for 12 hours or more a month, you can become a full voting Member of Bikerowave. That comes with all the benefits and privileges that go along with such an illustrious title.
