Swap Meet! Sunday December 11!

Posted on 11/27/2016 by rach150384

Bikerowave is hosting a swap meet! Come find some treasures this holiday season. We’ll be selling off some of our overstock and inviting others to do the same!

Our offerings will include pedals, saddles, brakes, derailleurs, cycling shoes, messenger bags, and more.

Swapmeet from 9 am ’til noon, followed by our regular Sunday shift (12-3 pm).

Coffee and donuts will be provided for the early birds!

Members of the public are welcome to sell bike-related items and can set up from 8:30 am onwards. Our location is 12255 Venice Boulevard, Los Angeles.
